Alex

Guitarist & Lead Vocalist, crafting sonic landscapes with meticulous precision and raw emotion.

The Melodic Architect

Alex, the melodic genius behind Jellytoes, is a true sound artist, painting with the tones of his guitars. His approach to music is both methodical and intuitive, combining technical precision with raw emotional expression.

He has more guitar gear than nearly anyone on the face of the earth, and each song is carefully crafted through an intricate signal chain—starting from his vast collection of vintage and custom guitars, running through an extensive array of pedals, and shaped with relentless precision.

"Every note must have intention. Every tone must have character. Music isn't just heard—it's felt."

His tone is an evolving masterpiece, constantly tweaked and refined, much like an espresso from a master of the craft. Alex's obsession is unmatched, ensuring that every note, chord, and melody carries the weight of intention and emotion.

When not performing with Jellytoes, Alex can be found in his home studio, experimenting with new sounds and recording techniques. His influences range from classic rock virtuosos to modern experimental composers, creating a unique sonic palette that defines the Jellytoes sound.
